# Testing Types & Methodology

- **External (ENPT):** security from the public internet; heavy OSINT (employees,
  emails, breached passwords).
- **Internal (INPT):** assessment from inside; Active Directory heavy.
- **Web app (WAPT):** OWASP-guided web testing.
- **Wireless (WPT):** depends on tech (Guest / WPA2-PSK / WPA2-Enterprise);
  ~4–8h per SSID plus 2–4h reporting.
- **Physical / social (PPT, PSET, PSA):** building entry, social engineering;
  humans are the weakest link.

## What makes a good tester

Technically capable; strong reporting and communication (technical **and**
non-technical); confident presenting findings at both levels during debriefs.
